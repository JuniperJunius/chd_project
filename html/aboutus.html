About Us
Welcome to CHD, acronym for Chitkara Help Desk. CHD is an online platform where hostellers and day scholars can file thier problems that they face in university on daily basis. This portal also allow staff to solve the queries raised by students. In this era of online world we would like to provide this helpful website. Hostellers being exhasted from all day long find difficult to visit reception to complain just a mere problem of a switch. Also, staff, being involved in the university chorus unintentionally fails to slove the query of some students. This leaves a loop hole in proper functioning of university with no one at fault. With this platform we would like to provide an easy way for both students and staff. Its easier for students to lodge their problems as everything is availabe on the same platorm. Same goes for staff as well as query by each student is present at CHD.
